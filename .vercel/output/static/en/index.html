Background

When I was in middle school, I began shooting portrait photography with my uncle's old Nikon D200. I loved the excuse it gave me to create, and I quickly became obsessed with the intersection between technology and art.

Nowadays, I'm a Computer Science and Digital Narrative and Interactive Design (DNID) double major at the University of Pittsburgh. While i'm mainly focussed on my degree, I have been dabbling in front-end web development when I have time off studying. It's a lot of fun, and I'm always looking for new projects to work on when I have the time... (never)

Ultimately, I want to create websites that I could see myself using every day.
